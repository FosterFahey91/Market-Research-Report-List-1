<p><p>The Tubular Membrane Filtration System market comprises several types, including Reverse Osmosis, Ultrafiltration, Microfiltration, and Nanofiltration. Reverse osmosis uses a semipermeable membrane to remove ions and molecules, ideal for desalination. Ultrafiltration separates larger particles and macromolecules, focusing on bacteria and larger solids. Microfiltration filters out smaller particles like pathogens, while Nanofiltration operates between reverse osmosis and ultrafiltration, effectively removing divalent ions and organic compounds. Each type serves distinct applications in water treatment, food processing, and industrial uses.</p></p>
