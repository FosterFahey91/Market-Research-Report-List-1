<p><p>Smoke exhaust fans are essential for maintaining air quality in buildings during fires. The centrifugal type fan utilizes an impeller to move air, providing high pressure and efficiency, making it suitable for larger spaces. Axial type fans, on the other hand, move air parallel to the fan shaft and are effective for ventilating smaller areas with lower pressure requirements. Other types may include mixed-flow and portable fans, catering to specific applications and environments, enhancing overall safety during emergencies.</p></p>
