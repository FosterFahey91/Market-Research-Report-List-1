<p><p>In the mining market, noise control systems are categorized into internal and external systems. Conversely, external noise control systems address noise pollution impacting surrounding communities through techniques such as vibration damping, noise barriers, and strategic equipment placement. Both systems aim to enhance safety, comply with regulations, and minimize environmental impact while promoting the well-being of workers and nearby residents.</p></p>
