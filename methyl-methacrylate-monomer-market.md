<p><p>The Methyl Methacrylate Monomer (MMA) market can be segmented based on production methods: the ACH method involves the reaction of acetone cyanohydrin with sulfuric acid, creating MMA with high purity; the Isobutylene method utilizes isobutylene and methacrylic acid, offering efficient production; and the Ethylene method employs ethylene in a polymerization process, allowing for versatility in chemical applications. Each method varies in cost, efficiency, and environmental impact, influencing their market presence and application in industries like plastics and coatings.</p></p>
