<p><strong>The Medical X-Ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li><li>Rotating Anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical X-ray tube market is primarily segmented into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode X-ray tubes. Stationary anode tubes feature a fixed target and are typically used in simpler imaging procedures due to their lower cost and compact design. Conversely, rotating anode tubes have a rotating target that disperses heat more effectively, allowing for higher output and improved image quality, making them ideal for complex diagnostic imaging applications. Both types serve crucial roles in medical diagnostics.</p></p>
