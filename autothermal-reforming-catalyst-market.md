<p><p>The Autothermal Reforming (ATR) catalyst market is divided into two main types: activated and non-activated catalysts. Activated catalysts are pre-treated to enhance their activity and selectivity, resulting in improved efficiency during the reforming process. They are often preferred for specific applications requiring higher performance. In contrast, non-activated catalysts are used in processes where less optimization is acceptable, typically leading to lower costs but potentially reduced efficiency. Both types play critical roles in hydrogen production and syngas generation.</p></p>
