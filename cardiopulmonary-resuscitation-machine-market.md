<p><p>The cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) machine market is divided into two primary types: mechanical CPR machines and automated CPR machines. Mechanical CPR machines provide consistent chest compressions through a mechanical device, allowing healthcare professionals to perform other critical tasks during resuscitation. Automated CPR machines, on the other hand, utilize advanced technology to deliver automated compressions, often adjusting the force based on real-time feedback. Both types enhance the efficacy and efficiency of CPR, improving patient outcomes in emergency situations.</p></p>
