<p><p>The commercial floor scrubber market comprises three main types: walk-behind floor scrubbers, ride-on floor scrubbers, and stand-on floor scrubbers. Walk-behind models are compact and ideal for smaller areas, allowing operators to scrub floors manually. Ride-on floor scrubbers enhance efficiency in larger spaces as operators ride on them, covering extensive areas quickly. Stand-on floor scrubbers combine features of both, offering compactness with the ability to stand while operating, making them versatile for varied operational demands in commercial settings.</p></p>
