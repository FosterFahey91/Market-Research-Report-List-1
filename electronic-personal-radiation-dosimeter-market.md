<p><p>The Electronic Personal Radiation Dosimeter (EPRD) market is divided into two main types: Active and Passive EPRDs. Active EPRDs continuously monitor and display radiation exposure in real-time, providing immediate feedback to users, making them ideal for environments with fluctuating radiation levels. Passive EPRDs, on the other hand, accumulate radiation data over time and require subsequent analysis for exposure assessment. Both types cater to different user needs in radiation safety, particularly in healthcare, nuclear, and research sectors.</p></p>
