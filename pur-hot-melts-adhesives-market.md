<p><p>The PUR Hot Melts Adhesives market is primarily classified into two types: Thermoplastic PUR Hot Melts Adhesives and Reactive PUR Hot Melts Adhesives. Thermoplastic PUR adhesives are characterized by their ability to be re-melted and reprocessed, offering flexibility and ease of application, making them ideal for packaging and woodworking. In contrast, Reactive PUR Hot Melts become chemically bonded upon curing, resulting in strong, durable joints suitable for demanding applications like automotive and construction, where enhanced performance and resistance are crucial.</p></p>
