<p><p>The fiberglass insulation market includes three main types: continuous fiber, fixed length fiber, and glass wool. Continuous fiber insulation consists of long strands woven together, providing excellent thermal performance and durability. Fixed length fiber insulation features shorter, uniformly cut fibers, making it easier to install in various spaces. Glass wool, made from molten glass spun into fibers, offers effective sound absorption and thermal resistance, commonly used in residential and industrial applications. Each type serves specific needs in insulation efficiency and application versatility.</p></p>
