<p><p>The CPAP mask market comprises three main types: Nasal Pillow Masks, Nasal Masks, and Full-Face Masks. Nasal Pillow Masks rest at the nostrils, providing a minimal interface and comfort, ideal for users who prefer less weight. Nasal Masks cover the nose, offering a balance between comfort and stability for moderate to severe sleep apnea. Full-Face Masks encompass both the nose and mouth, ensuring airway pressure delivery for mouth breathers, making them suitable for various therapy needs and preferences.</p></p>
