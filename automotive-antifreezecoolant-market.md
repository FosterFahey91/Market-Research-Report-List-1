<p><p>The automotive antifreeze/coolant market is primarily categorized into four types: ethylene glycol, propylene glycol, glycerin, and others. Ethylene glycol is the most widely used due to its effective heat transfer and freeze protection properties. Propylene glycol is favored for its non-toxicity, making it suitable for hybrid and electric vehicles. Glycerin serves as a more environmentally friendly option, while "others" include various blends and specialty coolants designed for specific applications, enhancing engine efficiency and longevity across different vehicle types.</p></p>
