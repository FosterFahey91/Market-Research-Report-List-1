<p><ul><li>Hot-Pressed Isotropic Neodymium Iron Boron Magnet</li><li>Hot Extrusion Anisotropic Neodymium Iron Boron Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot-pressed magnet market primarily includes two types: hot-pressed isotropic neodymium iron boron magnets and hot extruded anisotropic neodymium iron boron magnets. Isotropic magnets are uniformly magnetized in all directions, making them suitable for various applications where multidirectional magnetization is beneficial. In contrast, anisotropic magnets are magnetized in a specific direction, providing higher magnetic strength and efficiency, making them ideal for specialized applications like electric motors and generators. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
