<p><strong>The VR and AR Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waveguide</li><li>Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VR and AR optics market comprises two primary types: waveguides and lenses. Waveguides utilize thin, transparent layers to direct light, enabling compact, lightweight designs ideal for wearable devices. They offer a broad field of view and can integrate seamlessly into headsets. Lenses, on the other hand, are traditional optical elements that focus light and provide clear images. They tend to be bulkier but offer higher optical quality and performance, catering to a variety of consumer and industrial applications.</p></p>
