<p><p>The flight recorders market consists of two primary types: Flight Data Recorders (FDR) and Cockpit Voice Recorders (CVR). FDRs capture critical flight data, including speed, altitude, and trajectory, allowing for thorough analysis post-incident. Conversely, CVRs record audio from the cockpit, capturing pilot communications and ambient sounds, which are crucial for understanding the human factor during flights. Together, these devices enhance aviation safety by providing essential information for accident investigation and operational oversight.</p></p>
