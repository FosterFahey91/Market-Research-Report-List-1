<p><strong>The Immunochromato Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorption Method</li><li>Fluorescence Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunochromatography reader market comprises two primary types: absorption methods and fluorescence methods. Absorption methods rely on the binding of antigens to specific antibodies on a test strip, providing colorimetric results based on the intensity of the visual signal. In contrast, fluorescence methods utilize fluorescent labels that emit light when excited, allowing for high sensitivity and quantification of the analyte. Both methods are essential in point-of-care diagnostics, facilitating rapid and accurate detection of various health conditions.</p></p>
