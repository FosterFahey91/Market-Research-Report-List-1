<p><p>The Rubber Asphalt Distributor market includes two main types: Drag and Self-Propelled. Drag-type distributors are towed by another vehicle, making them cost-effective and suitable for smaller projects or areas with limited space. Self-Propelled distributors are standalone machines with their own engines, offering greater mobility, efficiency, and precision for larger-scale projects. Both types are used to evenly distribute rubberized asphalt for road construction, ensuring durability and performance, but differ in operation, capacity, and application scope.</p></p>
