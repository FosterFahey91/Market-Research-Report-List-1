<p><p>The high-voltage switchgear market primarily comprises two types: Gas Insulated Switchgear (GIS) and Air Insulated Switchgear (AIS). GIS utilizes gas as an insulating medium, offering a compact design and increased reliability, making it suitable for urban settings and space-constrained environments. In contrast, AIS relies on the air as an insulating medium, typically requiring more space but being cost-effective for outdoor applications. Both technologies play crucial roles in power distribution and electrical substation applications, enhancing system efficiency and safety.</p></p>
