<p><p>The dialysis catheters market comprises two main types: long-term hemodialysis catheters and short-term hemodialysis catheters. Long-term catheters are designed for extended use, typically for patients requiring ongoing dialysis over weeks or months, providing reliable access to blood vessels. In contrast, short-term catheters are intended for temporary use, generally for days to weeks, often utilized in emergency settings or acute cases. Both types play crucial roles in managing renal failure and ensuring effective dialysis treatment.</p></p>
