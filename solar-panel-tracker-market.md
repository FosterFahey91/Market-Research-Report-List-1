<p><ul><li>Single-axis Tracker</li><li>Dual-axis Tracker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar panel tracker market is divided into two main types: single-axis and dual-axis trackers. Single-axis trackers rotate on one axis, typically aligning panels with the sun's daily path, enhancing energy capture by 25-35%. Dual-axis trackers, which adjust on two axes, follow the sun's path more precisely throughout the day and across seasons, providing even greater efficiency gains, often exceeding 40%. The choice between these systems depends on installation costs, site characteristics, and desired energy output.</p></p>
