<p><p>Wireless charging systems for electric vehicles (EVs) are categorized into two main types: dynamic and stationary charging systems. Dynamic wireless charging systems enable EVs to charge while in motion, using embedded technologies in road infrastructure, enhancing convenience and reducing range anxiety. In contrast, stationary wireless charging systems involve charging when the vehicle is parked, using inductive charging pads or platforms. Both systems aim to streamline the charging process and improve the overall efficiency and adoption of electric vehicles in the market.</p></p>
