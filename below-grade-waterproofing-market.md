<p><ul><li>Bitumen</li><li>Bentonite</li><li>Rubberized Asphalt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The below-grade waterproofing market includes various types of materials: Bitumen, Bentonite, and Rubberized Asphalt. Bitumen provides a strong, flexible barrier against water intrusion, commonly used in membranes and coatings. Bentonite, a natural clay material, swells when wet, forming an impermeable seal that is effective in foundation applications. Rubberized Asphalt combines the flexibility of rubber with the waterproofing properties of asphalt, offering enhanced durability and resistance to cracking. Each type is tailored to specific environmental conditions and construction requirements.</p></p>
