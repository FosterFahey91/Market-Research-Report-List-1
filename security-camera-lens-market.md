<p><p>The security camera lens market comprises three main types: fixed lens, varifocal lens, and zoom lens. Fixed lenses offer a set focal length, providing consistent image quality but limited versatility. Varifocal lenses allow users to adjust the focal length for different viewing angles, making them adaptable for various environments. Zoom lenses provide a wider range of focal lengths, enabling distant objects to be brought into focus without moving the camera, thus enhancing monitoring capabilities across varying scenarios.</p></p>
