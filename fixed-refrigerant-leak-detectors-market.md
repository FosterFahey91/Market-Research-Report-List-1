<p><p>Fixed refrigerant leak detectors are crucial for monitoring refrigerant levels in HVAC systems. The market is segmented into two primary types: Halide and Electronic detectors. Halide detectors use a sensor that reacts to refrigerants, producing a visual indicator when a leak is detected. They are often simpler and cost-effective. Electronic detectors, on the other hand, utilize advanced technology for higher sensitivity and real-time monitoring, providing precise data and alerts, making them suitable for larger or more complex systems.</p></p>
