<p><ul><li>Particleboard (PB)</li><li>Medium density fibreboard (MDF)</li><li>High density fibreboard (HDF)</li><li>Oriented strand board (OSB)</li><li>Plywood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood-based furniture panel market comprises various types, each tailored for specific applications. Particleboard (PB) is an affordable option made from wood chips and adhesives, commonly used in low-cost furniture. Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF) offers a smooth surface and is ideal for intricate designs. High Density Fibreboard (HDF) is denser and more durable, suitable for flooring and high-stress furniture. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) features layered strands for strength, often used in structural applications. Plywood, made from thin wood veneers, combines flexibility and durability for versatile uses.</p></p>
