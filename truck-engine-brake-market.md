<p><strong>The Truck Engine Brake Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Release Brake</li><li>Exhaust Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Truck Engine Brake Market** includes two primary types: **Compression Release Brake** and **Exhaust Brake**. A **Compression Release Brake** works by releasing compressed air in the engine cylinders during the compression stroke, creating resistance that slows the vehicle. An **Exhaust Brake** restricts exhaust flow, increasing backpressure in the engine, which generates braking force. Both systems reduce wear on traditional brakes, enhance safety on steep descents, and improve vehicle control, with compression release brakes typically offering stronger braking power compared to exhaust brakes.</p></p>
