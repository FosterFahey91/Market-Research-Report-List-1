<p><p>The aluminium locking carabiners market can be categorized into three types: non-locking, manual locking, and auto-locking. Non-locking carabiners are simple and lightweight, suitable for quick connections but lack safety features. Manual locking carabiners require users to twist or slide to lock, providing increased security for activities like climbing or rescue operations. Auto-locking carabiners automatically engage and secure when clipped, offering hands-free operation and enhanced safety, making them ideal for professional or high-risk environments where reliability is crucial.</p></p>
