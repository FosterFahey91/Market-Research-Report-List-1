<p><p>The Milk and Dairy Analyzer market can be categorized into two primary types: ultrasonic and infrared analyzers. Ultrasonic analyzers utilize sound waves to measure properties like fat content and density, offering fast and non-invasive analysis. In contrast, infrared analyzers rely on light absorption at specific wavelengths to determine components such as protein and lactose. Both technologies are essential for ensuring quality control in dairy production, helping producers meet industry standards and consumer demands efficiently.</p></p>
