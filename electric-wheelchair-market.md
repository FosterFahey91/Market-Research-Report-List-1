<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Wheelchair Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Battery Electric Wheelchairs</li><li>Wet-Battery Electric Wheelchairs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric wheelchair market is primarily divided into two types based on battery systems: dry-battery and wet-battery electric wheelchairs. Dry-battery electric wheelchairs utilize sealed lead-acid or lithium-ion batteries, offering advantages such as lighter weight, compact size, and lower maintenance. Conversely, wet-battery electric wheelchairs use flooded lead-acid batteries, which are generally heavier and require regular maintenance but provide longer runtimes and are often more cost-effective. Both types cater to diverse user needs and preferences in mobility solutions.</p></p>
