<p><p>Automated Passenger Counting (APC) systems are essential for transportation management. Infrared type systems use infrared beams to detect the presence of passengers as they board or alight. Time-of-Flight type systems measure the time taken by light signals to bounce back, accurately counting passengers based on their movement. Stereoscopic Vision type systems utilize dual cameras to create depth perception, allowing for precise 3D passenger tracking. Together, these technologies enhance data collection for operational efficiency and service planning in public transport.</p></p>
