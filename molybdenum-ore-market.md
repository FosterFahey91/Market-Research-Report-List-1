<p><strong>The Molybdenum Ore Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Molybdenite (MoS2)</li><li>Associated Ore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molybdenum ore market primarily comprises two types: primary molybdenite (MoS2) and associated ore. Primary molybdenite, the primary source of molybdenum, is extracted largely from molybdenum-specific deposits, leading to higher quality and purity. Associated ore, on the other hand, contains molybdenum as a byproduct of mining for other metals, such as copper. These two market types reflect different extraction methods and supply dynamics, impacting pricing, production processes, and overall molybdenum availability in the market.</p></p>
