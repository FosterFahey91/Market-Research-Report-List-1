<p><strong>The Bag On Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aerosol BOV</li><li>Standard BOV</li><li>Non-Spray/Low Pressure BOV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bag On Valves (BOV) market consists of three main types. Aerosol BOVs utilize pressurized gas to dispense contents in a spray form, commonly seen in cosmetics and personal care products. Standard BOVs, often used for food and pharmaceuticals, provide a consistent and hygienic delivery of products without direct contact with the propellant. Non-Spray/Low Pressure BOVs are designed for gentle application, ideal for sensitive formulations like creams and lotions, allowing for a controlled release without aerosolization.</p></p>
