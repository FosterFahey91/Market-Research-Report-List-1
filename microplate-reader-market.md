<p><strong>The Microplate Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microplate reader market is categorized into two primary types: optical filter microplate readers and optical grating microplate readers. Optical filter readers utilize specific filters to isolate wavelengths for fluorescence, absorbance, or luminescence measurements, offering straightforward operation and precise detection. In contrast, optical grating readers employ diffraction gratings to disperse light into its spectral components, enabling faster readings across a wider wavelength range, enhancing flexibility in diverse applications. Both types serve critical roles in laboratories, driving advancements in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
