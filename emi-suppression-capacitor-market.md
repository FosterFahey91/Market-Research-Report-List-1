<p><p>The EMI suppression capacitor market primarily includes Class X and Class Y capacitors, each serving distinct purposes in electrical circuits. Class X capacitors are designed for across-the-line applications, protecting against high-frequency noise and allowing for voltage spikes. They are typically used in phase-to-phase applications. In contrast, Class Y capacitors are used for line-to-ground applications, ensuring safety by preventing electric shock. Both types are essential in enhancing electromagnetic compatibility and ensuring the reliability of electronic devices.</p></p>
