<p><p>The Rebar Coating Market primarily includes Fusion Bonded Epoxy Coated rebar and Cement-Polymer Composite Coated (CPCC) rebar. Fusion Bonded Epoxy Coated rebar features a durable epoxy layer that enhances corrosion resistance, making it suitable for harsh environments. In contrast, CPCC rebar combines cement and polymer to create a composite coating that offers excellent mechanical properties and resistance to chemical attack. Both coatings extend the lifespan of steel reinforcement in construction, ensuring structural integrity and reducing maintenance costs.</p></p>
