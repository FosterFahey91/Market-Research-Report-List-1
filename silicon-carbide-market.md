<p><p>Silicon carbide (SiC) market comprises three main types: 3C-SiC, 4H-SiC, and 6H-SiC. 3C-SiC, or cubic silicon carbide, features a zinc-blende crystal structure, offering beneficial electronic properties for high-frequency applications. 4H-SiC, with a hexagonal structure, is widely used in power electronics due to its thermal and electrical efficiency. 6H-SiC, another hexagonal variant, provides high breakdown voltage and is utilized in high-temperature environments. Each type caters to specific applications in industries like automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
