<p><ul><li>Flow Cell Based System</li><li>Direct Read System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The discrete chemistry analyzer market comprises two main types: flow cell-based systems and direct read systems. Flow cell-based systems utilize a continuous fluidic pathway, allowing for simultaneous analysis of multiple samples while minimizing contamination risks. In contrast, direct read systems analyze samples without the need for extensive sample preparation, providing rapid results. Both types cater to clinical laboratories and research settings, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in biochemical analyses and supporting a wide range of diagnostic applications.</p></p>
