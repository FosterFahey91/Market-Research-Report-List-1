<p><p>The polysulfone resin market encompasses three main types: Polysulfone (PSU), Polyarylsulfone (PES), and Polyphenylsulfone (PPSU). PSU is known for its exceptional thermal stability and mechanical properties, making it suitable for applications in the aerospace and automotive sectors. PES offers higher temperature resistance and enhanced chemical stability, ideal for demanding environments. PPSU exhibits superior toughness and hydrolytic resistance, commonly utilized in medical devices and food processing. Each type serves distinct applications based on their unique performance characteristics.</p></p>
