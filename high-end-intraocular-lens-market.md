<p><p>The high-end intraocular lens market consists primarily of spherical and aspherical lenses, designed for cataract and refractive surgeries. Spherical lenses have a uniform curvature, offering consistent vision correction but may produce some spherical aberrations. In contrast, aspherical lenses feature varying curvature to minimize aberrations and enhance image quality, particularly in low-light conditions. Aspherical designs are increasingly preferred for their superior optical performance, driving market growth as patients seek enhanced visual outcomes following eye surgeries.</p></p>
