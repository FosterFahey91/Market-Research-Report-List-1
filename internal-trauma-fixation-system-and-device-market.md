<p><p>The Internal Trauma Fixation System and Device market encompasses various types designed for stabilizing fractures internally. Internal Fixation Plates are metal plates affixed to bones with screws, providing structural support. Internal Fixation Screws are utilized to secure bone fragments together, ensuring alignment. Internal Fixation Nails are rods inserted into the medullary cavity of long bones, promoting healing through intramedullary support. Other Internal Fixation devices include specialized systems like tension bands and wire constructs that assist in fracture stabilization through innovative methods.</p></p>
