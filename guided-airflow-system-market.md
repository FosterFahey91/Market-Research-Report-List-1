<p><p>The Guided Airflow System market encompasses two key types: Mixing Ventilation and Parallel Flow Ventilation. Mixing Ventilation involves blending air in a space to maintain uniform temperature and air quality by distributing conditioned air throughout the environment. In contrast, Parallel Flow Ventilation directs air in a more uniform manner along a linear path, minimizing turbulence while maximizing efficiency. Both systems aim to enhance indoor air quality and energy efficiency, catering to diverse building requirements and occupancy needs.</p></p>
