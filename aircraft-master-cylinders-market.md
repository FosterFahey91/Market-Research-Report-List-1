<p><p>The Aircraft Master Cylinders Market is typically divided into two primary types: push-type and pull-type. Push-type master cylinders operate by pushing hydraulic fluid to activate the hydraulic system, commonly used in various aircraft braking systems. In contrast, pull-type master cylinders create hydraulic pressure by pulling the piston, typically applied in systems where space efficiency is crucial. Each type serves specific applications in aerospace engineering, addressing varying design requirements and operational functionalities within aircraft systems.</p></p>
