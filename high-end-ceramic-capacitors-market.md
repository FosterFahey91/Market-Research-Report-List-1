<p><ul><li>Class 1 Ceramic Capacitors</li><li>Class 2 Ceramic Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end ceramic capacitors market is categorized into Class 1 and Class 2 capacitors. Class 1 ceramic capacitors, made with materials like C0G and NP0, offer superior stability and low loss, making them ideal for high-frequency applications. Class 2 capacitors, including X7R and Y5V types, have higher capacitance values and are used in applications where temperature and voltage variations are less critical, but they exhibit greater capacitance shifts with environmental changes. Each class serves distinct performance needs in electronic devices.</p></p>
