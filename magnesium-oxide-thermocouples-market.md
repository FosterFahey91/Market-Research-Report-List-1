<p><p>Magnesium oxide thermocouples are categorized into three main types based on installation and application requirements. Grounded type thermocouples feature a metallic connection to the sensor, providing rapid response times, making them ideal for dynamic processes. Ungrounded types are electrically insulated, offering high accuracy and stability, suitable for applications with varying electrical noise. Exposed types have their sensing elements directly exposed to the environment, allowing for quick temperature readings, particularly in gases or liquids. Each type serves specific industrial needs, enhancing temperature measurement efficiency.</p></p>
