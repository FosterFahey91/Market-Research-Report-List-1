<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Sensor</li><li>Active Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel speed sensor market comprises two main types: passive and active sensors. Passive sensors generate a signal from the magnetic field created by the wheel's movement, often using a toothed wheel and an inductive pickup. Active sensors, on the other hand, utilize a built-in power source and produce a digital signal, providing more precise readings. Both types play crucial roles in traction control, anti-lock braking systems, and vehicle stability, enhancing overall safety and performance in modern vehicles.</p></p>
