<p><p>The Medical Breathable PE Tape market is primarily categorized into two types: LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LDPE is known for its flexibility and softness, making it ideal for sensitive skin applications, while HDPE offers increased durability and strength, suitable for environments requiring robust adhesives. Both types ensure breathability, allowing moisture and air to pass through, which is crucial for wound care. The choice between LDPE and HDPE often depends on the desired balance between comfort and durability.</p></p>
