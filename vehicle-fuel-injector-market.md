<p><p>The Vehicle Fuel Injector market comprises three main types: Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI), Port Fuel Injectors (PFI), and Diesel Fuel Injectors. GDI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency and power. PFI sprays fuel into the intake manifold, ensuring thorough mixing with air, which is effective for lower emissions. Diesel Fuel Injectors are designed to handle high pressures, optimizing fuel delivery in diesel engines for improved performance and fuel efficiency, catering to different engine requirements and technological advancements.</p></p>
