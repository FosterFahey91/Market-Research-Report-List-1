<p><p>The Vertical Cavity Surface Emitter Laser (VCSEL) market is primarily divided into two types: Single Mode VCSEL and Multimode VCSEL. Single Mode VCSELs operate with a single spatial mode, offering high precision and efficiency, making them ideal for communication applications, such as data centers and long-distance transmission. In contrast, Multimode VCSELs support multiple spatial modes, capable of higher power output and broader bandwidth, which is suitable for short-distance applications like optical interconnects and sensors. Both types play crucial roles in various technological advancements.</p></p>
