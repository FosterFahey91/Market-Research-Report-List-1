<p><p>The cheese ingredients market is divided into two primary types: natural and processed cheeses. Natural cheeses, including varieties like Cheddar, Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Gouda, are made from milk through methods that maintain their original flavors and textures. They are often sought for their rich taste and health benefits. In contrast, processed cheeses are manufactured through blending natural cheese with emulsifiers, salts, and other additives to achieve a uniform texture and extended shelf life, making them convenient for various culinary applications.</p></p>
