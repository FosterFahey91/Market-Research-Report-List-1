<p><p>The Nuclear Medicine Imaging Equipment market includes Full Ring PET Scanners and Partial Ring PET Scanners, both used for positron emission tomography. Full Ring PET Scanners consist of a complete circular detector array, providing high-resolution images and improved sensitivity for detailed diagnostics. In contrast, Partial Ring PET Scanners feature a segment of a ring, which allows for a more compact design and is often used in specific applications. Both types facilitate advanced imaging crucial for monitoring various medical conditions.</p></p>
