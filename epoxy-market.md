<p><p>The epoxy market can be divided into three main types: liquid, solid, and solution. Liquid epoxy is versatile and widely used in coatings, adhesives, and composite applications due to its ease of application and strong bonding properties. Solid epoxy typically comes in pre-cured forms, used in applications requiring robust mechanical strength, such as flooring and structural components. Solution epoxy, often dissolved in solvents, provides flexibility in formulation and is commonly used in paints and coatings for enhanced application properties.</p></p>
