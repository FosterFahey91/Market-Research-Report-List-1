<p><p>The Transdermal/Transmucosal Drug Delivery System market encompasses two primary types: Transdermal Drug Delivery Systems and Transmucosal Drug Delivery Systems. Transdermal systems deliver therapeutic agents through the skin for systemic circulation, offering advantages like improved patient compliance and bypassing first-pass metabolism. Conversely, Transmucosal systems utilize mucosal surfaces (like the oral, nasal, or rectal membranes) for drug absorption, providing rapid onset of action. Both systems enhance bioavailability and patient convenience, making them increasingly popular in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
