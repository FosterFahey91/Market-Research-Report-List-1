<p><p>Electric Vehicles (EVs) encompass various market types based on their power sources. Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) run purely on electricity stored in batteries, offering zero emissions and lower operating costs. Plug-in Electric Vehicles (PEVs) include both BEVs and plug-in hybrids, allowing for electric-only driving with a gasoline engine for longer ranges. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor, optimizing fuel efficiency while reducing emissions. Each type supports the transition to sustainable transportation, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
