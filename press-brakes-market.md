<p><p>The press brake market comprises four main types: hydraulic, mechanical, pneumatic, and servo motor. Hydraulic press brakes utilize fluid power for bending, offering high force and versatility. Mechanical press brakes rely on a crank mechanism, providing speed and precision but less flexibility. Pneumatic press brakes use compressed air to operate, suitable for lighter applications. Servo motor press brakes combine electric drives with advanced controls, offering energy efficiency and high precision, making them increasingly popular for modern manufacturing needs.</p></p>
