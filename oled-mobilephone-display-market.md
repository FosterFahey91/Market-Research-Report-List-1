<p><strong>The OLED Mobilephone Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PMOLED</li><li>AMOLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED mobile phone display market primarily consists of PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) and AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) technologies. PMOLED displays are simpler and suitable for smaller screens, using a grid layout for control, which limits scalability and refresh rates. In contrast, AMOLED features an active matrix of thin-film transistors, enabling faster refresh rates and higher resolutions, making it ideal for larger and more complex displays. This technological differentiation influences their applications, performance, and market demand.</p></p>
