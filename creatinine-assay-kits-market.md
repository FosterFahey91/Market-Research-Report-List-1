<p><p>The creatinine assay kits market includes various testing methods to measure creatinine levels in biological samples. The Jaffe's Kinetic Test is a traditional colorimetric method utilizing alkaline picrate for detection, known for its speed. Creatinine-PAP (Particle-enhanced immunoassay) offers enhanced specificity and sensitivity by utilizing antibodies to detect creatinine. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) provides a quantitative measurement through antigen-antibody interactions, often used for more comprehensive analyses in clinical and research settings. Each method has distinct advantages for different applications.</p></p>
