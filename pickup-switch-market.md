<p><ul><li>Touch Type</li><li>Press Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pickup switches in the market are primarily categorized into two types: touch type and press type. Touch type switches are activated by a light touch, allowing for quick and responsive operation, often found in applications requiring precision. In contrast, press type switches require a deliberate push to activate, providing tactile feedback and ensuring accidental activation is minimized. Both types serve various applications, from musical instruments to automotive systems, catering to different user preferences and operational needs.</p></p>
