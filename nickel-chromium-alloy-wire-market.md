Round wire is commonly used in heating elements and resistors due to its uniform cross-section and efficient conductivity. Flat wire is favored in applications requiring high surface area, such as transformers and inductors. Square wire offers enhanced structural integrity and is used in specialized applications, including aerospace and electronics, where strength and precision are paramount. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
