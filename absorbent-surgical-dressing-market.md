<p><p>The absorbent surgical dressing market encompasses various product types used in wound care. Primary dressings are applied directly to the wound, facilitating healing and fluid management. Secondary dressings provide additional support and protection over primary dressings. Absorbents are materials that draw moisture away from the wound, enhancing comfort. Bandages secure dressings in place, while adhesive tapes ensure stability. Protective dressings shield wounds from external contaminants, promoting a safe healing environment. Each type plays a critical role in effective wound management.</p></p>
