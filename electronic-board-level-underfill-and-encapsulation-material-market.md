<p><strong>The Electronic Board Level Underfill and Encapsulation Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>No Flow Underfill</li><li>Capillary Underfill</li><li>Molded Underfill</li><li>Wafer level Underfill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Board Level Underfill and Encapsulation Material market includes several types: </p><p>1. **No Flow Underfill** involves materials that do not flow into gaps easily, providing excellent thermal and mechanical properties.</p><p>2. **Capillary Underfill** uses capillary action to fill spaces between components, ensuring uniform coverage.</p><p>3. **Molded Underfill** is applied as a mold, allowing precise placement and minimizing voids.</p><p>4. **Wafer Level Underfill** is applied directly on wafers to enhance reliability before packaging, reducing defects in advanced semiconductor devices.</p></p>
