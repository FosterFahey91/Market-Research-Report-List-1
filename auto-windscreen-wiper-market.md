<p><p>The Auto Windscreen Wiper market includes two main types: Boneless Wipers and Bone Wipers. Boneless Wipers feature a sleek, one-piece design without a metal frame, offering better aerodynamics, quieter operation, and improved contact with the windshield for enhanced performance. Bone Wipers, or traditional wipers, use a metal frame with multiple pressure points, making them durable and cost-effective but less efficient in harsh weather. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, balancing performance, durability, and affordability in varying driving conditions.</p></p>
