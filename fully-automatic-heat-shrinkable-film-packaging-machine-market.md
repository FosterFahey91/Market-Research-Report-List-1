<p><p>The fully automatic heat shrinkable film packaging machine market can be categorized into two primary types: Vertical Form Fill Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form Fill Seal (H-FFS). V-FFS machines produce bags from a flat film, filling and sealing them in a vertical orientation, ideal for granular products. Conversely, H-FFS machines work horizontally, accommodating various product shapes, making them suitable for larger or bulk items. Both types enhance efficiency and automation in packaging, optimizing production workflows across diverse industries.</p></p>
