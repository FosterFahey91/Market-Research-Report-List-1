<p><strong>The Automotive Cabin Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat-Pump Cabin Heater</li><li>PTC Cabin Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive cabin heaters market features two primary types: heat-pump cabin heaters and PTC cabin heaters. Heat-pump cabin heaters use a refrigeration cycle to extract heat from the outside air, making them energy-efficient, especially in mild climates. In contrast, PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) cabin heaters utilize ceramic heating elements that provide rapid warmth through electrical resistance. PTC heaters are known for their reliability and ability to heat quickly, making them suitable for a variety of driving conditions.</p></p>
