<p><ul><li>Active Thermal Infrared Detector</li><li>Passive Thermal Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The potable infrared detector market features two primary types: active and passive thermal infrared detectors. Active thermal infrared detectors emit their own infrared signals to detect heat sources, enhancing sensitivity and responsiveness. In contrast, passive thermal infrared detectors sense the infrared radiation emitted naturally by objects, making them ideal for applications like surveillance and fire detection without requiring external energy. Both types serve critical roles in safety, security, and environmental monitoring, catering to diverse industrial and residential needs.</p></p>
