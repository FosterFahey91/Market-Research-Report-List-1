<p><p>RNA Isothermal Amplification Technology includes two primary market types: the endpoint method and real-time fluorescence detection. The endpoint method involves analyzing the amplification results after the reaction is complete, typically using gel electrophoresis or colorimetric assays. In contrast, real-time fluorescence detection allows for continuous monitoring of the amplification process, providing immediate results through fluorescent signals. Both methods enhance the sensitivity and specificity of RNA analysis, catering to various applications in diagnostics and research, thereby driving market growth.</p></p>
