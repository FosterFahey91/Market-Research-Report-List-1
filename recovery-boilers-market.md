<p><p>The recovery boiler market encompasses three main types: Soda Recovery Boilers, Kraft Recovery Boilers, and Chemical Recovery Boilers. Soda Recovery Boilers primarily recycle sodium compounds from the pulping process in the production of soda ash, while Kraft Recovery Boilers focus on recovering chemicals, heat, and energy in the kraft pulping process. Chemical Recovery Boilers, on the other hand, serve to reclaim various chemicals used in different industrial processes. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing sustainability and resource efficiency in the pulp and paper industry.</p></p>
