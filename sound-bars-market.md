<p><p>The sound bar market is primarily divided into two types: active and passive sound bars. Active sound bars are self-contained units equipped with built-in amplifiers and speakers, providing an all-in-one audio solution ideal for home theaters. They typically include features like Bluetooth connectivity and various sound modes. In contrast, passive sound bars require an external amplifier or receiver to power them, allowing for more customization and integration into existing audio systems, appealing to audiophiles seeking enhanced audio performance.</p></p>
