<p><p>The ICU beds market consists of three main types: electric beds, semi-electric beds, and manual beds. Electric beds are fully operated by motors, allowing for precise adjustments in height and position with minimal effort. Semi-electric beds combine motorized functionalities for head and foot adjustments while requiring manual changes for height. Manual beds rely entirely on mechanical mechanisms, requiring staff to make adjustments physically. Each type serves specific patient needs, emphasizing comfort, accessibility, and ease of use for healthcare providers.</p></p>
