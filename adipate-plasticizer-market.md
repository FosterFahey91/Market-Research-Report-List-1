<p><p>The adipate plasticizer market consists of various types, each tailored for specific applications. Diisononyl adipate (DINA) offers high stability and is favored in plasticizing applications for food packaging. Diisodecyl adipate (DIDA) is known for its superior durability and is used in automotive and industrial applications. Other adipate types include specialty formulations that cater to niche applications, enhancing performance and flexibility in diverse industries.</p></p>
