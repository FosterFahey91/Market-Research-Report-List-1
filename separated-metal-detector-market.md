<p><p>The separated metal detector market can be categorized into three main types: electromagnetic induction, X-ray, and microwave detectors. Electromagnetic induction detectors utilize changing magnetic fields to identify metal objects, making them effective for various applications. X-ray detectors employ ionizing radiation to visualize metal items within objects, commonly used in security and industrial settings. Microwave detectors, on the other hand, utilize microwave frequencies to sense metallic materials, providing high sensitivity and non-invasive detection capabilities across diverse environments.</p></p>
