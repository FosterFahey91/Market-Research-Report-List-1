<p><ul><li>Biologic Dural Repair</li><li>Synthetic Dural Repair</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dural repair market encompasses two primary types: biologic and synthetic dural repair solutions. Biologic dural repair utilizes natural materials, such as collagen or human-derived tissue, which promote healing and integration with surrounding tissues. Conversely, synthetic dural repair involves artificial materials, such as polymers or non-absorbable membranes, designed for durability and mechanical support. Both types aim to prevent cerebrospinal fluid leaks and facilitate recovery after neurosurgical procedures, addressing the specific needs of patients and surgical outcomes.</p></p>
