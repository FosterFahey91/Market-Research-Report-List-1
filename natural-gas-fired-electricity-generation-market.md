<p><strong>The Natural Gas-Fired Electricity Generation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCGT</li><li>OCGT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural gas-fired electricity generation encompasses two main market types: Combined Cycle Gas Turbines (CCGT) and Open Cycle Gas Turbines (OCGT). CCGT systems efficiently utilize waste heat from gas turbines to produce additional electricity via steam turbines, making them suitable for base-load generation. OCGT systems, on the other hand, are simpler and faster to start, making them ideal for peak-load generation. Both technologies offer flexibility and lower emissions compared to coal, driving the transition to cleaner energy sources.</p></p>
