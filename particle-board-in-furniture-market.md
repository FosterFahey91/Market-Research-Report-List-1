<p><ul><li>Fire-rated</li><li>Moisture-resistant</li><li>Standard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particle board is a widely used engineered wood product in the furniture market, created by compressing wood particles and adhesives. It is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Fire-rated particle board** is treated to resist ignition and minimize flame spread, making it suitable for commercial spaces requiring enhanced safety.</p><p>2. **Moisture-resistant particle board** is designed for areas with high humidity, such as kitchens and bathrooms, preventing warping and degradation.</p><p>3. **Standard particle board** is the general-purpose option, ideal for lower-cost furniture but with limited durability against moisture and heat.</p></p>
