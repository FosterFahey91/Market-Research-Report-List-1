<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Antilock Braking System Modulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-channel ABS</li><li>Two-channel ABS</li><li>Three-channel ABS</li><li>Four-channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Antilock Braking System (ABS) modulator market is segmented based on the number of channels, which determine how braking force is distributed across wheels. One-channel ABS operates a single wheel, primarily suited for lightweight vehicles. Two-channel ABS controls braking for the front and rear wheels separately, enhancing stability. Three-channel ABS is typically used in light trucks, managing front wheels independently while sharing rear braking. Four-channel ABS provides independent control for all four wheels, offering optimal safety and performance in various driving conditions.</p></p>
