<p><strong>The Digital Smart Valve Positioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioners</li><li>Double Acting Positioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital smart valve positioners are essential components in process control, offering precise regulation of valve positions. The market is categorized into single-acting and double-acting positioners. Single-acting positioners utilize a spring mechanism to control the valve's position in one direction, relying on external pressure for the opposite motion, making them simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, double-acting positioners exert force in both directions, providing enhanced control and responsiveness, ideal for high-demand applications requiring greater accuracy and reliability in valve operations.</p></p>
