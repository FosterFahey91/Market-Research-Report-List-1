<p><p>The thermoform packaging market encompasses various materials including PET, PVC, PP, PE, PS, aluminum, and paper & paperboard. PE (Polyethylene) is flexible and cost-effective, whereas PS (Polystyrene) provides rigidity and insulation. Aluminum is favored for its barrier properties and recyclability. Paper and paperboard are eco-friendly options known for their versatility and sustainability in packaging solutions.</p></p>
