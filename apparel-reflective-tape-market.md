<p><p>The apparel reflective tape market primarily consists of two types: glass beads materials and microprismatic materials. Glass beads materials utilize tiny glass spheres to reflect light, providing high visibility for safety apparel. In contrast, microprismatic materials employ a complex prism structure that enhances light reflection, offering superior visibility and durability. Both types cater to various industries, including construction, emergency services, and outdoor recreation, ensuring users are seen in low-light conditions while promoting safety and compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
