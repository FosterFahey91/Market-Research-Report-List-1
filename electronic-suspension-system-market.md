<p><p>The **Electronic Suspension System** market includes **Semi-Active** and **Active Suspension Systems**. **Semi-Active Systems** use sensors to adjust damping forces in real-time, improving ride comfort and stability without external energy input. **Active Suspension Systems** employ actuators and advanced controls to actively adjust suspension components, offering superior handling and comfort by counteracting road irregularities. Both systems enhance vehicle performance, with semi-active being cost-effective and active providing higher precision and adaptability. They cater to varying consumer needs in automotive comfort and dynamics.</p></p>
