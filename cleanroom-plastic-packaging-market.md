<p><p>Cleanroom plastic packaging includes various materials, each serving specific functions. Polyethylene (PE) is flexible and widely used for its moisture barrier properties. Polypropylene (PP) offers higher heat resistance and is often chosen for sterile applications. Polystyrene (PS) is lightweight and provides good insulation, making it suitable for lightweight packaging. Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) is valued for its durability and clarity, often used when visibility of the contents is essential. Each type is tailored to meet the stringent requirements of cleanroom environments.</p></p>
