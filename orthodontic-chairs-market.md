<p><p>The orthodontic chairs market is categorized into electric and electromechanical types. Electric orthodontic chairs offer seamless height and position adjustments, powered by electric motors, enhancing precision and efficiency during treatments. They often include advanced features like built-in lighting and digital controls, improving patient comfort and clinician accessibility. On the other hand, electromechanical orthodontic chairs utilize a combination of mechanical systems and electric components, offering reliable performance with less complexity, making them a cost-effective choice for many dental practices.</p></p>
