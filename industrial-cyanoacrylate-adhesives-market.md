<p><p>The industrial cyanoacrylate adhesives market is categorized into three viscosity types: low, medium, and high viscosity. Low viscosity adhesives are thin and rapidly penetrate surfaces, ideal for bonding small gaps and delicate materials. Medium viscosity adhesives offer a balance between flow and control, suitable for general-purpose applications. High viscosity adhesives, which are thicker, provide enhanced gap-filling capabilities and greater control during application, making them suitable for larger components and demanding environments. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
