<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Vascular Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare-Metal Stents</li><li>Drug-Eluting Stents</li><li>Bioabsorbable Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vascular stents market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Bare-Metal Stents (BMS)** are made of stainless steel or similar materials, providing structural support without any additional therapeutic coating.  </p><p>2. **Drug-Eluting Stents (DES)** are coated with medication that is gradually released to prevent restenosis, promoting better long-term outcomes.  </p><p>3. **Bioabsorbable Stents** gradually dissolve in the body over time, providing temporary support while allowing natural vessel healing, ultimately eliminating the need for permanent implants. Each type addresses specific clinical needs and patient profiles.</p></p>
