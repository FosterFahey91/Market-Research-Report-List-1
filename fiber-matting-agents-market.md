<p><p>The Fiber Matting Agents market is divided into two primary types: organic matting agents and inorganic matting agents. Organic matting agents, derived from natural substances, enhance surface texture and reduce gloss while often being environmentally friendly. In contrast, inorganic matting agents, typically composed of minerals like silica, offer excellent durability and resistance to chemicals and UV radiation. Both types serve essential roles in various industries, including coatings, plastics, and inks, by improving product appearance and performance.</p></p>
