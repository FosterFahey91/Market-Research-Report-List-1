<p><p>The Medical PVC Adhesive Tapes market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided tapes feature adhesive on one side, ideal for securing dressings and securing medical devices. In contrast, double-sided tapes contain adhesive on both sides, facilitating the bonding of materials without exposing any sticky surface, making them suitable for applications like wound management and prosthetic fittings. Both types offer flexibility, comfort, and durability, significantly contributing to patient care in various medical settings.</p></p>
