<p><strong>The Brushed Direct Current Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Wound Brush DC Motors</li><li>Permanent Magnet Brush DC Motors</li><li>Shunt Wound Brush DC Motors</li><li>Compound Wound Brush DC Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brushed direct current (DC) motor market features several types, including series wound, permanent magnet, shunt wound, and compound wound motors. Series wound motors have windings connected in series with the load, providing high starting torque. Permanent magnet motors use permanent magnets for field excitation, offering compact designs and efficiency. Shunt wound motors have field windings connected in parallel, allowing for stable speed under varying loads. Compound wound motors combine features of both series and shunt types, delivering versatile performance across applications.</p></p>
