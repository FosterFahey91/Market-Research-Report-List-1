Coils are burned to release insect-repelling smoke; vaporizers emit repellent vapors using electricity or heat. Mats contain active ingredients that evaporate slowly when heated. Aerosols are spray-based repellents for direct application on skin or surfaces, while creams provide a lotion-like option for personal use. Each type varies in usage, convenience, and effectiveness, catering to different consumer preferences and environments.</p></p>
