<p><p>The parallel twin screw extruder market is divided into two main types: co-rotating and counter-rotating. Co-rotating extruders have both screws rotating in the same direction, promoting efficient mixing and uniform material processing, making them ideal for complex formulations and additives. In contrast, counter-rotating extruders have screws rotating in opposite directions, offering better control over material flow and higher shear rates, which is advantageous for specific applications that require precise material characteristics. Both types serve distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
