<p><p>The side thrusters market encompasses various types designed to enhance vessel maneuverability. Tunnel side thrusters are installed within hull tunnels, providing efficient thrust with minimal drag. Externally mounted side thrusters are affixed to the vessel's exterior, offering flexibility in placement and installation. Waterjet side thrusters utilize high-velocity water jets for thrust, delivering exceptional control and responsiveness. Each type caters to specific operational needs, enabling vessels to navigate tight spaces and improve handling in challenging marine environments.</p></p>
