<p><strong>The Collapsible Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack Assist Type (REPS)</li><li>Colum Assist Type (CEPS)</li><li>Pinion Assist Type (PEPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Collapsible Electric Power Steering (EPS) market includes three main types: Rack Assist Type (REPS), Column Assist Type (CEPS), and Pinion Assist Type (PEPS). REPS utilizes an electric motor on the steering rack, providing direct assistance to the wheels and enhancing responsiveness. CEPS integrates the motor in the steering column, allowing for a more compact design and lighter weight. PEPS, with the motor located on the pinion gear, offers a balance between reliability and performance. Each type caters to different vehicle requirements and driving experiences.</p></p>
