<p><p>The surveillance camera lens market comprises three main types: fixed lenses, varifocal lenses, and zoom lenses. Fixed lenses offer a set focal length, providing a consistent field of view, ideal for static monitoring. Varifocal lenses allow users to adjust the focal length within a specified range, offering flexibility in capturing varying scenes. Zoom lenses enhance this capability further by enabling remote adjustments, allowing operators to change both focal length and perspective without repositioning the camera, enhancing surveillance adaptability.</p></p>
