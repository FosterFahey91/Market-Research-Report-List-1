<p><p>The conical implant market encompasses various types, primarily categorized into root implants and plate implants. Root implants are designed to mimic natural tooth roots, providing stable support for crowns and bridges, and are typically used in areas with adequate bone density. In contrast, plate dental implants are flat and broad, ideal for patients with limited bone height, offering a wider surface area for support. Both types aim to enhance dental functionality and aesthetics, addressing diverse patient needs in modern dentistry.</p></p>
