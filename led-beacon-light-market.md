<p><ul><li>High Intensity Lights (HIL)</li><li>Medium Intensity Lights (MIL)</li><li>Low Intensity Lights (LIL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED Beacon Light market is categorized into three types based on intensity: High Intensity Lights (HIL), Medium Intensity Lights (MIL), and Low Intensity Lights (LIL). HILs are used for high-visibility applications, such as aviation and maritime, to signal obstacles. MILs provide moderate visibility for applications like construction zones and traffic management. LILs are suitable for low-traffic areas, offering basic signaling for safety without the brightness of HILs or MILs, catering to various regulatory needs and safety standards.</p></p>
