<p><p>MEMS inertial sensors encompass accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers, which are crucial for measuring motion and orientation in various applications. Accelerometers detect changes in velocity and tilt, enabling features like screen orientation in devices. Gyroscopes measure angular velocity, providing stability and navigation support, particularly in drones and smartphones. Magnetometers sense magnetic fields, aiding in compass functionality and location tracking. These sensors are increasingly integrated into consumer electronics, automotive systems, and industrial applications, driving market growth through enhanced sensing capabilities and miniaturization.</p></p>
