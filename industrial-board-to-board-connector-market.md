<p><strong>The Industrial Board-to-Board Connector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-hole Technology</li><li>Surface-mount Technology</li><li>Plug-in Technology</li><li>Solderless Stacking Mezzanine Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Board-to-Board Connector market features several technology types. Through-hole technology involves connectors inserted into a PCB with leads soldered on the opposite side, offering robust mechanical stability. Surface-mount technology allows connectors to be mounted directly on the surface of PCBs, enabling a compact design. Plug-in technology facilitates easy connections and disconnections, suitable for modular systems. Solderless stacking mezzanine technology connects multiple PCBs without soldering, improving assembly efficiency and flexibility in industrial applications.</p></p>
