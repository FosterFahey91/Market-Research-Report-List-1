<p><ul><li>Single Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors</li><li>Multi-Wire Coolant Temperature Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Car Coolant Temperature Sensor market includes Single Wire and Multi-Wire types. Single Wire sensors use one wire to transmit temperature data to the engine control unit (ECU), relying on grounding through the engine block. Multi-Wire sensors have additional wires for power, ground, and signal, offering more accurate and stable readings. Both types monitor engine coolant temperature, ensuring optimal performance and preventing overheating. Single Wire sensors are simpler and cost-effective, while Multi-Wire sensors provide enhanced precision and reliability for modern vehicles.</p></p>
