<p><p>The blood collection tubes market is categorized into several types based on their specific functions. Serum separating tubes facilitate the separation of serum from blood after clotting, making them ideal for biochemical tests. EDTA tubes, containing anticoagulant, preserve blood for hematology tests by preventing clotting. Plasma separation tubes are designed to separate plasma and are used for various tests requiring plasma analysis. Other tubes include those with different additives for specific applications, catering to diverse diagnostic needs in clinical laboratories.</p></p>

<p><p>The blood collection tubes market is crucial for venous and capillary blood collection applications. Both methods cater to various healthcare settings, emphasizing the importance of efficient, safe, and reliable blood collection solutions for diagnostics and treatment monitoring.</p></p>
