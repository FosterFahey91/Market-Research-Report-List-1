<p><p>The 3-phase distribution boards market comprises various types, including Main Distribution Boards (MDBs) and Emergency Distribution Boards (EDBs). MDBs serve as the central point for distributing electricity from the supply source to various circuits, ensuring efficient load management and protection. In contrast, EDBs are designed to provide power during outages, featuring backup systems that maintain critical operations. Both types are essential for ensuring reliable electrical supply in commercial, industrial, and residential settings, enhancing safety and operational continuity.</p></p>
