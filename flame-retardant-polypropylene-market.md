<p><p>The Flame Retardant Polypropylene market is broadly categorized into two types: Halogen Flame Retardant PP and Halogen-Free Flame Retardant PP. Halogen Flame Retardant PP incorporates halogen compounds, effectively inhibiting combustion but raising environmental and health concerns due to toxic byproducts. In contrast, Halogen-Free Flame Retardant PP uses non-halogenated compounds, presenting a safer alternative with reduced environmental impact while maintaining effective fire resistance. Both types serve critical applications across various industries, including automotive, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
