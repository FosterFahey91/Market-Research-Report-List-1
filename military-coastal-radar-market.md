<p><p>The military coastal radar market primarily consists of X-Band and S-Band radar systems, each with distinct applications. X-Band radar operates at higher frequencies, providing enhanced resolution and target discrimination, making it ideal for surveillance and tracking small, fast-moving objects. In contrast, S-Band radar, with a lower frequency, offers longer-range capabilities and is effective in adverse weather conditions, making it suitable for monitoring larger maritime areas. Both types play crucial roles in national security and coastal defense strategies.</p></p>
