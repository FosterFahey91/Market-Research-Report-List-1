<p><p>Pipe tracing systems are essential for maintaining temperature in pipelines, particularly in industries like oil and gas, chemicals, and food processing. Steam tracing utilizes steam to provide consistent heating along pipes, preventing freezing and maintaining product quality. In contrast, electric tracing employs electrical heat tracing cables to regulate temperatures, offering more precise control and energy efficiency. Both types are crucial for enhancing process reliability, reducing downtime, and ensuring safety in environments where temperature management is critical.</p></p>
