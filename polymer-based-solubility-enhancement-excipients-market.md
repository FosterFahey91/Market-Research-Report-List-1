<p><p>The polymer-based solubility enhancement excipients market consists of two main types: solid dispersion and particle size reduction. Solid dispersion involves dispersing a drug in a polymer matrix to improve its solubility and bioavailability. This method enhances drug release rates and stability. On the other hand, particle size reduction focuses on decreasing the particle size of a drug, which increases the surface area and enhances solubility. Both methods are essential for improving the efficacy of poorly soluble pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
