<p><p>The wood varnishes market is primarily categorized into water-based, oil-based, solvent-based, and others. Water-based varnishes are known for their quick drying time, low odor, and easy cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. Oil-based varnishes offer durability and a rich finish but have longer drying times and stronger odors. Solvent-based varnishes provide excellent adhesion and resistance to moisture but can emit volatile organic compounds. The "others" category includes specialized formulations, such as polyurethane varnishes, that cater to specific applications and performance needs.</p></p>
