<p><ul><li>A-flute</li><li>B-flute</li><li>C-flute</li><li>E-flute</li><li>F-flute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated fiberboard market comprises various flute types, each tailored for specific applications. A-flute offers excellent cushioning and insulation, ideal for fragile items. B-flute is thinner, providing a smooth surface for printing, making it suitable for retail packaging. C-flute strikes a balance between cushioning and strength, commonly used for shipping boxes. E-flute is lightweight and offers a flat surface, perfect for small boxes and point-of-purchase displays. F-flute is the thinnest, designed for lightweight packaging and intricate printing options.</p></p>
