<p><p>The porous silicon substrates market is categorized into three main types: microporous, mesoporous, and macroporous silicon substrates. Microporous silicon features tiny pores, enhancing surface area for applications like sensors and drug delivery. Mesoporous silicon has larger pores, offering improved drug loading and release capabilities, ideal for biomedical uses. Macroporous silicon, with even larger pores, is suited for applications requiring structural support and fluid flow, such as in filtration systems and tissue engineering, catering to various industries' needs.</p></p>
