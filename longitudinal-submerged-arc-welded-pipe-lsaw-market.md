<p><p>Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded (LSAW) pipes are categorized primarily into three types based on their manufacturing processes: the JCOE process, the UOE process, and other methods. The JCOE process involves forming a steel plate into a "J" shape, then expanding it to a "C" before finalizing the "O" shape, ensuring strong welds. The UOE process entails U-shaped and O-shaped forming of plates, offering high strength and precise dimensions. Other market methods may include variations like double submerged arc welding, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
