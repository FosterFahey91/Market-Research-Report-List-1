<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Pressing Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NdFeB bonded magnet market primarily consists of two types: injection molding and pressing molding. Injection molding involves melting NdFeB compounds and injecting them into molds, allowing for intricate shapes and efficient mass production. This method is ideal for complex geometries and high-volume applications. Pressing molding, on the other hand, utilizes pressure to form the magnet from a mixture of NdFeB powder and a binding agent, resulting in denser magnets suitable for specific performance requirements. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
