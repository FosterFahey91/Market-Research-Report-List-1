<p><p>The polymeric film for separation market comprises various types, including hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes feature fine fibers that maximize surface area for filtration, making them suitable for gas and liquid separation. Spiral wound membranes consist of flat sheets wound around a central tube, offering compact designs for large-scale applications, particularly in desalination. Other types may include flat sheet and tubular systems, each tailored for specific separation processes, such as wastewater treatment or industrial filtrations.</p></p>
