<p><strong>The Transparent Evaporated Barrier Packaging Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AlOx Type</li><li>SiOx Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transparent evaporated barrier packaging films are essential for preserving product integrity by minimizing moisture and oxygen transmission. The AlOx type, which incorporates aluminum oxide, offers excellent barrier performance, particularly against moisture, making it suitable for food and pharmaceutical applications. On the other hand, the SiOx type utilizes silicon oxide, providing superior oxygen barrier properties and is often used for sensitive electronics and active packaging. Both types cater to different industry needs, enhancing product shelf life and quality.</p></p>
