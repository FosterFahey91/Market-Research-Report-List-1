<p><strong>The RGB CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Global Shutter</li><li>Rolling Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RGB CMOS image sensor market is primarily divided into two types: global shutter and rolling shutter. Global shutter sensors capture an entire image simultaneously, preventing motion distortion and producing high-quality images in dynamic environments, making them ideal for applications like filmmaking and machine vision. In contrast, rolling shutter sensors capture images line-by-line, which can lead to motion artifacts but allows for faster frame rates, making them suitable for smartphone cameras and surveillance systems. Both types cater to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
