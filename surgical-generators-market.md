<p><p>Surgical generators are essential devices used in medical procedures to produce high-frequency electrical energy for tissue cutting and coagulation. The market is primarily divided into two types: Ground Referenced Generators and Isolated Generators. Ground Referenced Generators utilize the ground as a reference point for electrical safety, making them suitable for various surgical applications. In contrast, Isolated Generators provide electrical isolation, reducing the risk of unintended current paths and enhancing patient safety, especially in sensitive environments.</p></p>
